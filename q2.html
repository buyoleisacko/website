Young people should develop plans which will allow them to make most effective decisions. Young people should stop wasting so much time on social media, they should sleep early and wake up early consistently so that they may be able to accomplish more during the day.
Several studies have correlated waking up early with success. Using time wisely, young people need to plan their time wisely by planning to accomplish the most important or time-sensitive tasks first.
Self development is the key, it is impossible without good time management. Attending to priorities, by prioritizing their days and this will allow them to handle emergencies that will eventually pop up.
The youths should evaluate themselves,self evaluation is important at any stage in life and can be done by monitoring the tasks that they have to do.
Staying calm, youths should develop the ability to stay calm under pressure situations and this will make them less likely to suffer from the effects of stress,anxiety and worry.Calm approaches will lead to better health and higher levels of productivity.
